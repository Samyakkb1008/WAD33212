About Chhava

This is the about us page.

  • Chhava is a Hindi historical action film released on February 14, 2025, directed by Laxman Utekar. It is based on the life of Maratha king Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj and is an adaptation of the Marathi novel "Chhava" by Shivaji Sawant.
  • Chhava: A historical drama based on the life of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj, son of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The film highlights Sambhaji Maharaj's courage and warrior spirit.
